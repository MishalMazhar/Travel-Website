Our Policies

Your trust matters. Please review our policies before booking your trip.

🔒 Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and ensure your personal information is never shared without consent. Data is used only to improve your booking and travel experience.

📜 Terms & Conditions

By booking a trip with TravelExplorer, you agree to abide by our guidelines, respect local cultures, and follow travel safety instructions provided by our team.

🍪 Cookie Policy

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze website traffic. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

💳 Cancellation & Refund Policy

Cancellations made 7 days before the trip are eligible for a full refund. Cancellations within 7 days will incur a 50% fee. No refunds for cancellations within 48 hours.

🛡 Travel Safety Policy

Your safety is our top priority. All tours follow government regulations, certified guides, and safety equipment standards. We recommend all travelers have valid insurance.

🔐 Data Protection & Security

Our website uses SSL encryption to protect your data. Payment information is processed securely through trusted third-party gateways.

👥 User Responsibilities

Travelers must carry valid identification, respect cultural values, and comply with local laws. Misconduct may result in cancellation of services without refund.
